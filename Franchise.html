Royalty @3%

We are charging a royalty of 3% on the products that you buy from us. Let us say, if you buy 10 kgs of Tea Powder from us which is worth of Rs. 3500, we will add 3% to the 3500/- and you pay 3605/-. Likewise, for every product that you buy from us, a royalty @3% will be added to the bill. Be assured that we will not ask any royalty on the gross sales that a unit makes.
