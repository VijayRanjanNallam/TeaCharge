Overview of Tea Charge


Not too long ago, four friends met in a city for a cup of tea. Although they found the taste ordinary, the ambience of the shop did not please them. They explored the city in pursuit of the best quality tea shop, but much to their disappointment, they couldn’t find any. They studied the needs of the local tea consumers and decided to craft the perfect venue that offered the finest quality of tea with a pleasant ambience and the top notch service to the customers. This thought birthed the idea of “Tea Charge”- an enlivening space to recharge your soul.



What do we serve at Tea Charge?

At Tea Charge, our customers experience a unique taste which our chefs developed and mastered over the years. Our tea powder is the perfect blend of tea leaves and tea dust garnished with a pinch of our love. Our USP is the method of preparation which gives the perfect taste to fulfil the customer’s palette without burning their “pocket”.



What is special about our Teas?

  • We select our tea-making products that produce the highest quality of tea.
  • Our teas are carefully crafted and processed in different methods to provide a unique taste.
  • We use paramount quality and 100% pure spices.
  • We practice the finest preparation norms to ensure that the best quality of tea is served.
